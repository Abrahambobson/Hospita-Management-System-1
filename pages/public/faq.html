Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How do I book an appointment? ▼
You can book an appointment by logging into your patient portal and navigating to the appointments section. Select your preferred doctor, date, and time, then confirm your booking. You can also call our reception desk at +1 (555) 123-4567 to book over the phone.
What should I bring for my first visit? ▼
Please bring a valid ID, insurance card, and any medical records from previous treatments. If you have any prescription documents or test reports, please bring those as well. Fill out our new patient form 15 minutes before your appointment.
Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment? ▼
Yes, you can reschedule or cancel your appointment through your patient portal or by contacting our reception desk. Please provide at least 24 hours notice for cancellations. Cancellations made within 24 hours may incur a fee.
What are your visiting hours? ▼
Our visiting hours are Monday-Friday: 8 AM - 8 PM, Saturday: 9 AM - 6 PM, and Sunday: 10 AM - 4 PM. ICU and emergency wards have restricted visiting hours. Please check with the reception desk for specific departmental hours.
How can I access my health records? ▼
Your complete health records are available in your patient portal 24/7. Log in with your credentials to view your medical history, test results, prescriptions, and doctor's notes. You can also request printed copies from our medical records department.
Do you accept insurance? ▼
Yes, we accept most major insurance providers. Please verify your coverage with your insurance company before your visit. Our billing department can also help confirm your coverage. We also offer flexible payment plans for uninsured patients.
What is your emergency protocol? ▼
In case of emergency, call 911 or come directly to our emergency room. Our emergency team is available 24/7 to provide immediate care. You can also use the emergency contact feature in your patient portal to alert our medical staff.
How do I request a prescription refill? ▼
You can request prescription refills through your patient portal, by calling your doctor's office, or by visiting the pharmacy counter. Most refills are processed within 24-48 hours. For urgent needs, contact our pharmacy directly.
How is my personal health information protected? ▼
We comply fully with HIPAA regulations and use advanced encryption to protect your data. Your health information is stored securely and only accessible to authorized healthcare providers involved in your care. We never share your information without your consent.
Do you offer telemedicine/virtual appointments? ▼
Yes, we offer telemedicine services for follow-up consultations and non-emergency appointments. Select "Virtual Appointment" when booking through our patient portal. You'll receive a video call link before your appointment time.
