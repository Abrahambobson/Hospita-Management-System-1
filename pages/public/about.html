Our History

Founded in 1995, our hospital has grown from a small 50-bed facility to a comprehensive healthcare center with over 500 beds. Over the past 28 years, we have served hundreds of thousands of patients and earned a reputation for quality care and innovation in medical practice.

Our journey has been marked by continuous expansion, investment in technology, and training of our medical staff. Today, we operate 30+ specialized departments and employ over 200 dedicated healthcare professionals.
